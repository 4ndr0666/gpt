**Example Questions & Answer Key**

**Test-Taking Tips:**
Familiarize yourself with the NHIE study manuals & recommended resources.

**Key Techniques:**

* Recognize question types/formats.
* Identify keywords.
* Select the best possible answers.
* Use the process of elimination.

**Sample Questions:**

* Minimum clearance around electrical panels?

  * A. 30 inches wide & 36 inches deep, B. 24 inches wide & 30 inches deep, C. 36 inches wide & 24 inches deep
  * **Answer:** A

* What is the maximum slope for a horizontal drain pipe?

  * A. 1/4 inch per foot, B. 1/2 inch per foot, C. 1 inch per foot
  * **Answer:** A

* Which of the following is NOT considered a defect?

  * A. A flue liner with a small crack, B. Missing chimney cap, C. Creosote buildup
  * **Answer:** A

* What is the recommended insulation R-value for attic floors in Zone 5?

  * A. R-38, B. R-45, C. R-49
  * **Answer:** C

* What is efflorescence on basement walls typically caused by?

  * A. Poor indoor air circulation, B. Excessive indoor humidity, C. Water intrusion from exterior
  * **Answer:** C

* What is the minimum height requirement for light switches?

  * A. 24 inches, B. No minimum height requirement, C. 36 inches
  * **Answer:** B

* True or False: A double-tapped circuit breaker is typically acceptable if the breaker is designed to hold two wires.

  * **Answer:** True

* Why is venting a plumbing system necessary?

  * A. To prevent sewer gases from entering the home, B. To allow wastewater to flow freely, C. Both A and B
  * **Answer:** C

* What is a common concern with galvanized pipes?

  * A. High pressure capacity, B. Corrosion and possible reduced water flow, C. Excellent thermal insulation
  * **Answer:** B

* What is the recommended slope away from the foundation?

  * A. At least 6 inches within the first 10 feet, B. At least 4 inches within the first 10 feet, C. At least 2 inches within the first 10 feet
  * **Answer:** A

* What is the minimum stairway tread depth?

  * A. 10 inches, B. 9 inches, C. 11 inches
  * **Answer:** C

* True or False: Aluminum wiring is allowed for branch circuit wiring in residential construction.

  * **Answer:** True

* What are the requirements for a ridge board?

  * **Answer:** A ridge board is not required.

* What is the best recommendation for a crack at the upper corner on the lock side of an interior door that widens toward the ceiling & causes the door to scrape against the lock side?

  * **Answer:** Evaluation by an engineer.

* What is the difference in current that causes a GFCI device to trip?

  * **Answer:** 6 milliamperes.

* What are the minimum overhead electrical conductor clearances?

  * **Answer:** Walkway: 10 feet, Roadway: 18 feet, Deck: 10 feet.

* How many energized conductors supply a 240-volt, 200-amp, single-phase electric service?

  * **Answer:** 2.

* What should be installed when non-metallic sheathed wire is run through metal cut-outs in a metal-framed building?

  * **Answer:** Plastic grommets.

* What does a circuit breaker protect wiring from?

  * **Answer:** Current.

* What is the nominal electric voltage for three conductors at the weatherhead?

  * **Answer:** Single phase, 120/240 volts.

* What should the joints on the exterior of a brick chimney be?

  * **Answer:** Concaved.

* What is the most likely cause of significant efflorescence on a concrete block basement wall?

  * **Answer:** The soil has settled creating poor exterior drainage.

* What is the color of the flame from a properly adjusted oil burner?

  * **Answer:** White with Yellow Tips.

* What is the best example of heat flow by conduction?

  * **Answer:** Heat flowing from the water inside to the body of a radiator.

* What is the minimum width of stairways not considering railings?

  * **Answer:** 36 inches.

* What is the required elevation for an open flame or sparking device within fuel-burning appliances off the garage floor?

  * **Answer:** 18 inches.

* Which type of pipe is concerned with dezincification?

  * **Answer:** CPVC.

* What should you comment on when you find a toilet with a Sloan Flushmate tank?

  * **Answer:** Comment that this unit may be subject to a recall.

* What are the requirements for doors that are part of the barrier enclosure to a pool area?

  * **Answer:** They must have both audible & visual notification when activated.

* What is the minimum dimension for skip sheathing for wood shingles and shakes?

  * **Answer:** 1"x4".

* What is the primary purpose of control joints placed in concrete driveways?

  * **Answer:** To reduce random cracking.

* What are the components of I-joists?

  * **Answer:** Webbing.

* What is the purpose of floor joist bridging?

  * **Answer:** Provides lateral support.

* What is the depth of an egress window well that requires a ladder?

  * **Answer:** 44 inches.

* Where can roof truss uplift cause cracks to form?

  * **Answer:** At interior wall/ceiling intersections.

* Which set of building terms do not belong together?

  * A. Cripple, Trimmer, Purlin, B. Joist, Rafter, Stud
  * **Answer:** Purlin does not belong with Cripple and Trimmer.

* What should be done if a 3-inch deep notch is cut at the end of a 2x10 floor joist?

  * **Answer:** The notch exceeds maximum allowable depth & needs proper repair.

* What is the maximum length for a clothes dryer exhaust duct?

  * **Answer:** Up to 35 developed feet long.
